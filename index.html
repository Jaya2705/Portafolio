Why should I study Web Design?

The Web Designer profession is, like all those related to technology and new media, one of those commonly called "of the future". Today, most public and private companies have a website, just as many people have personal pages and there are businesses set up entirely on the Internet. For this reason, people who design, make and maintain them in a responsible and professional manner are necessary.
